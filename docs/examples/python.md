- [FFT](python-fft.md) — Per-instance 1-D and 2-D FFT with plan reuse.
- [Ring Buffers](python-buffers.md) — Double-mapped lock-free ring buffers for producer/consumer pipelines.
- [AGC](python-agc.md) — Closed-loop power controller with decimated loop update.
- [Corr & Detector](python-corr.md) — Cross-correlation and CFAR detection in 1-D and 2-D.
- [Detection Theory](python-detection.md) — Marcum Q closed-form curves and Monte Carlo validation.
- [2-D Acquisition](python-detection2d.md) — GPS/CDMA acquisition grid with Bonferroni-corrected CFAR.
