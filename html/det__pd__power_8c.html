Detection probability for the power detector.

Pd = Q_1(sqrt(2·dwell·snr_power), sqrt(2·power_threshold))

Parameters
snr_powerPer-sample power SNR (signal power / noise power at the correlator output, linear). 0 gives Pd = Pfa.
dwellCoherent integration depth; must be >= 1.
power_thresholdThreshold p, e.g. from det_threshold_power().
Returns
Detection probability in &#91;0, 1&#93;.

Example: det_pd_power(1.0, 4, det_threshold_power(1e-6)) ~ 0.78 (same result as det_pd(1.0, 4, det_threshold(1e-6)) since snr_power=1 corresponds to snr_amplitude=1 and the Q_1 arguments are identical)
